PITTSBURGH, April 6, 2026 /PRNewswire/ -- "I wanted to create modified optical frames that enable you to easily secure the straps of protective face masks," said an inventor, from Pittsview, Ala., "so I invented the MASK SLIT (EYEWEAR). My design provides added comfort, and it reduces irritation behind the ears."

The patent-pending invention provides an improved design for optical products that would offer a more comfortable way to wear a protective face mask. In doing so, it eliminates irritation behind the ears associated with loops or straps. As a result, it increases safety, comfort, and convenience. The invention features a practical design that is easy to wear so it is ideal for anyone who wears prescription glasses, sunglasses, reading glasses, and other optical products as well as face masks to lower the risk of respiratory virus transmission. Additionally, a prototype model and technical drawings are available upon request.

The original design was submitted to the Birmingham sales office of InventHelp. It is currently available for licensing or sale to manufacturers or marketers.
