What are the Factors That Have a Significant Contribution to the Growth of the microplate systems market?

The increased use of microplate systems has been driven by the growing demand for high-throughput screening in drug discovery, clinical diagnostics, and biomarker research, which is fueled by rising R&D spending and the need for quicker assay processing and greater efficiency.
