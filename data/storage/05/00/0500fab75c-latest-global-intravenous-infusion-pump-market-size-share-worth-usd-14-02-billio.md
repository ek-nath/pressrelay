What are the Factors That Have a Significant Contribution to the Growth of the intravenous infusion pump market?

The increase in chronic care patients who need long-term IV treatment including oncological care, diabetes care, and pain care, and the rise in patient hospitalization and the necessity of precise medication delivery, has increased the demand for infusion pump products. It has been reported that chronic diseases impact hundreds of millions of people across the world, and the number of cancer and diabetes cases still increases at a sharply rising rate. With the increasing elderly population, patients will also demand high quality and safe pumps to administer chemotherapy, parenteral nutrition and home infusion therapies.

The technological advancements have brought in intelligent pumps with AI-based dose error minimization software, electronic health record interoperability, wireless connectivity, and portability, thus enhancing the safety, precision and efficiency of clinicians. Other motivators are increased focus on preventing medication errors, more homecare and ambulatory treatments, positive reimbursement policies, and government programs that support advanced healthcare facilities in the developed and emerging markets.
