DURHAM, N.C., Jan. 06, 2026 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Bioventus Inc. (Nasdaq: BVS), a global leader in innovations for active healing, today announced that Rob Claypoole, Chief Executive Officer, will present at the 44th Annual J.P. Morgan Healthcare Conference on Wednesday, January 14, 2026, at 9:45 a.m. PT (12:45 p.m. ET).

The live webcast of the presentation, including Q&A, may be accessed on the Investors section of the Bioventus website and will be available for replay until February 14, 2026.
