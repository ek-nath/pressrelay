PITTSBURGH, April 6, 2026 /PRNewswire/ -- "I wanted to create one convenient product that can be used to clean and prepare an injection site upon the skin while also numbing the area," said an inventor, from Aurora, Colo., "so I invented the SOFT NEEDLES. My design would help reduce pain and discomfort for patients."

The invention provides an improved way to clean and numb areas of the skin. In doing so, it can be used when drawing blood, starting an IV, being vaccinated, or getting medicine. As a result, it helps reduce pain. It also provides peace of mind. The invention features a practical and convenient design that is easy to employ so it is ideal for medical facilities. Additionally, it could be packaged in various quantities.

The original design was submitted to the National sales office of InventHelp. It is currently available for licensing or sale to manufacturers or marketers.
