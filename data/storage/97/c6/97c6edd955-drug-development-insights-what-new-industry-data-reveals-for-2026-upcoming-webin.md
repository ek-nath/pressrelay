# Drug Development Insights: What New Industry Data Reveals for 2026, Upcoming Webinar Hosted by Xtalks

In this free webinar, learn how today's industry trends and challenges are shaping drug development in 2026, with insights to help navigate uncertainty and make faster, more strategic decisions.

TORONTO, March 11, 2026 /PRNewswire/ -- The future for drug developers is increasingly shaped by how effectively they adapt to complexity, change and rising pressure across the development landscape.

To understand how the industry is responding, the PPD™ clinical research business of Thermo Fisher Scientific surveyed 150 leaders at biotech and pharmaceutical organizations worldwide for the fourth annual Pulse industry report. This year's findings reveal a clear throughline: uncertainty is shaping decisions across R&D, from strategy and investment to execution.

In this webinar, industry experts will explore key signals from the 2026 Pulse research and discuss what they mean for navigating today's evolving environment. Attendees will gain perspective on how peers are adapting their approaches to remain agile, manage risk and continue advancing innovation.

How are leading organizations making confident decisions when conditions remain unpredictable? Register for this webinar to gain insights that help navigate 2026 drug development with greater clarity and strategic focus.

Join experts from the PPD™ clinical research business of Thermo Fisher Scientific, Daniel Jones, Vice President and General Manager, Internal Medicine Business Unit; and Praveen Nadella, Head of Customer Insights & Intelligence, for the live webinar on Tuesday, March 31, 2026, at 11am EDT (5pm CEST/EU-Central).
