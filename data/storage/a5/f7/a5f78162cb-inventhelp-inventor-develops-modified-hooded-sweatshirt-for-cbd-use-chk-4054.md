PITTSBURGH, April 6, 2026 /PRNewswire/ -- "I wanted to create a comfortable and convenient hoody that offers a hands-free way to store and use CBD products," said an inventor, from Homewood, Ill., "so I invented the C B D HOODIE. My design offers a functional and fashionable garment for individuals who use CBD recreationally or for medicinal purposes."

The invention provides a modified hooded sweatshirt for individuals who use CBD. In doing so, it offers a convenient and discreet means of using CBD. As a result, it increases comfort and convenience. It also eliminates the need to carry and keep track of a separate ignitor, mouthpiece, etc. The invention features a trendy design that is easy to wear and use so it is ideal for anyone who uses CBD recreationally or for medicinal purposes. Additionally, a prototype model and technical drawings are available upon request.

The original design was submitted to the Chicago sales office of InventHelp. It is currently available for licensing or sale to manufacturers or marketers.
